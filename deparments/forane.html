Borivali Forane

Borivali Forane consists of 4 churches, they are:

  • St.Thomas Forane church, Borivali
  • Little FLower church, Dahisar
  • St. Thomas church, Mira road
  • Sacred Heart chruch, Bhayander

    • These four chruch are clubbed together and represented in the Kalyan Dioceses as Borivali Forane. Borivali Forane has it's own Forane council committee under which there are committees of Pithruvedi, Mathrusangam and Youth who actively sync with the parish associations for representing particular departments to the Diocese.

    St.Thomas Catholic church Borivali situatued on a serene ad beautiful hilliock in the heart of I.C Colony is a house of prayer and a spiritual refuge for more than 1200 Syro Malaber Catholics.

    Initiated as a small community in 1980, Sacred Heart Church has now grown to have over 270 families who zealously participated in strengthening the faith handed over by St. Thomas, the apostle.

    Karunya trust Social Worker Volunteer ID card distributed to Mr. Lijo Joseph and Mr. Martin KJ considering the efforts taken to feed the under previlaged and destitutes on road in last year lock down during pandemic .
