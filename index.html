To prosper in evangelistic goals of delivering the faith of our forefathers to the current generation by adapting to the changing times.

Initiated as a small community in 1980, Sacred Heart Church has now grown to have over 270 families who zealously participated in strengthening the faith handed over by St. Thomas, the apostle. From the time of its inception to the current day, with the help of Vicars and parishioners, Sacred Heart Church has strived to explore avenues to reach out spiritually and contribute to the social development of the community.
